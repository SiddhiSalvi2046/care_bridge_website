Guidelines & Process

Before You Donate

  • Eat a healthy, low-fat meal.
  • Drink plenty of water (500ml+).
  • Get 8 hours of sleep.
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before.

Collection Protocol

  • Present Doctor’s prescription.
  • Provide Patient ID & Blood Report.
  • Processing time: 30–60 mins.
  • Replacement donor may be requested.

The Donation Journey

1. Registration: Fill out basic health and contact details.
2. Medical Check: Quick check of Hb level, BP, and weight.
3. Donation: The actual process takes only 8–10 minutes.
4. Refreshment: Rest for 10 mins with a light snack and juice.
